This is where I store solutions and notes for [JavaScript and Algorithms and Data Structures Masterclass](https://www.udemy.com/course/js-algorithms-and-data-structures-masterclass) by Colt Steele.
I use TypeScript (with Deno) and lots of notes and explanations are my own.

I do TDD for everything, but I don't include the test files in the text because it takes a ton of extra space.
You can take a look at the tests directly from the repo on Gitlab though:

* https://gitlab.com/devhowto/algorithms-and-data-structures
